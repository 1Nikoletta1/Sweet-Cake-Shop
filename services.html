Our services

Lead time: we require a lead time of at least 3 days for standard orders, and 7 days for customised orders.

Delivery: $15 for orders below $100, free for orders above $100. Sentosa & Jurong Island deliveries subject to $15 surcharge. A 10-minute waiting time applies to every delivery. Items not collected within this period will be subject to a $10 re-delivery fee. Self-collection is also available at our shop.

Payment methods: Paypal, credit/debit cards or bank transfer.

Enquiries: sweet-cake-shop@gmail.com or use the Contact Us form

Loyalty Points: if you have accumulated any loyalty points from our previous loyalty programme, email us once you've set up an account on this website and we will port your points over.

Closure Dates:

25th December: Deliveries between 10am-12pm and 2pm-4pm only. Self-collections from 11am-2pm only.

January: Closed 1-7 Jan, self-collection/deliveries resume on 8 Jan (Fri).

February: Closed 8-9 Feb, self-collection/deliveries resume on 10 Feb (Wed). Closed 29 Feb to 6 Mar, self-collection/deliveries resume on 7 Mar (Mon).

Frequently asked questions

1. Do you customise cakes? Yes, we do - but only with buttercream, artificial dye (only upon request) and icing printing. We do not use fondant in our cakes! We also do tiered buttercream cakes. If you would like to customise a cake, please drop us a note at sweet-cake-shop@gmail.com. Thanks!

2. Are your cakes very sweet? We've reduced the sugar content in all of our cakes and baked goods. However, certain flavours like Salted Caramel - by its nature - tend to be sweeter than the rest. The level of sweetness is subjective so please be the judge of it. :)

3. Do you deliver cakes? Yes, we deliver cakes - with no minimum order. However, we use an external courier so we will need to add on a delivery fee. Please note that the courier will only wait 10 minutes at your delivery address so it would be great for someone to be on standby to receive the cake. Otherwise, the courier will charge a redelivery fee.

4. Do you cater to events? Yes, we cater to company events and weddings.

5. Loyalty Points: if you have accumulated any loyalty points from our previous loyalty programme, email us once you've set up an account on this website and we will port your points over.

6. Is this a home business? No, we can't bake legally from home in Singapore. We bake from an NEA-licensed kitchen, and our staff are armed with the food handling certificate from NEA.
